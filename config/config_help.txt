When editing the config.txt file DO NOT EDIT ANYTHING TO THE RIGHT OF THE COLONS.

Setting Bot Commander Role: Put the name of the role you want to be the Bot 
Commander role to the right of the colon next to "commander". It is case sensitive.
    * EXAMPLE: commander:Bot Commander

Configuring Shop: in shop.txt, on each line put the name of the rank and the cost of
that rank in the format of "name of rank:cost". The rank name is case sensitive.
    *EXAMPLE: Bogan1:100
              Bogan2:200
